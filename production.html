Our creative process

PRODUCTION PROCESS

Our current process is based on years of experience and learning: reflecting on the times that we got it wrong, doing more of what we get right and constantly reviewing and refining. Every decision that’s formed our creative approach is based on one of these factors.

1. CREATIVE

Great creative is priceless. This is where we define your content strategy. We’ll ask you plenty of questions to gain crucial insight into your business and market.

  • Discovery Workshops
  • Concept Development
  • Scriptwriting
  • Storyboarding

2. PRE-PRODUCTION

In our opinion, this is the most important step in the process. During pre-production, we lay the groundwork for the perfect shoot, taking care of locations, casting, and scheduling. We handle everything needed to turn our storyboards into reality.

  • Production Management
  • Broadcast Clearance for TV and Radio
  • Location Scouting and Shooting Permissions
  • Talent Sourcing
  • Props and Wardrobe

3. PRODUCTION

All the hard work within the planning stages is put into action. Our crew arm themselves with the best cameras and video tech available. Actors don their wardrobes, and it’s time for the magic to happen.

  • Video Production Crew
  • Production Equipment
  • Photography

4. POST-PRODUCTION

The job isn’t finished when the cameras stop rolling. A slick post-production treatment is what turns footage into the finished product. We take care of everything that elevates your production to the next level, including editing, colour grading, graphics and audio mixing by masters of their craft.

  • Video Editing
  • Colour Grading
  • Sound Design and Music
