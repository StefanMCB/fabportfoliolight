Talent management services

Our core is finding the right talent for your brand

Your One-Stop-Solution to optimize your talent marketing activities when it comes to brand fit, monitoring of cost and usage rights.

01. Talent strategy
Strategy & Brand building

Finding the right talent & collaboration partner enabling and pushing the best performance.

  • Defining core brand equity items or potential approaches for brands or products
  • Potential brand building & product development
  • Defining key campaign objectives & mandatories / ideation
  • Define financial framework & ROI objectives
  • Calibrating concept / message with existing brand codes
  • Briefing talent/partner requirements & codes (including topic affinity, tonality, emotionality, etc.)
02. Creative casting
Research/Casting

Access to maximum choice of talent, creator & performance data to setup the most authentic cast and potential concept to match the brand.

  • Talent casting (influencers, celebrities, storytellers, models, actors, artists, featured extras) independent & transparent
  • For Social Media: Talent Insights / Setcard creation – aligned with marketing goals / KPIs / brand attributes
  • For classic media: Talent PDF & E-Castings / Live-Video Casting globally / Streetcasting
  • For Social Media on top: Brand & talent matching conception due to closeness to talent & management (valuable insights to use for ideation & campaign)
03. Relationship management
Handling & Contracting

Complete operational management of licensing negotiation, talent handling and contracting for corporates and brand development deals.

  • Analysis (price, market, talent, usage rights) • Optimization of rights (terms, territory) + corresponding recommendation
  • Negotiation (initial contract / prolongations in case of expiring rights) / asset calculation
  • Best price/share guarantee after transparent brief/Package Deals (25–30 % reduction)
  • Customized contracts talent & partnerships or support only
  • Onset talent handling if wished (VIP)
04. Social media
Content

Operational management of content conception & creation together with our strong partners

  • Activity steering
  • Synchronization of marketing activities; interface to overall digital communication
  • Guidelines according to client needs (post concepts & formats)
  • Artist Pool Building (including social media creator networks – drone-filmmakers etc.)
  • Snackable Content Production within efficient budgets (stills, video, all social media formats)
05. Negotiation & Licensing purchase
Controlling

For Social Media: Success measurement and reporting

  • KPI tracking / ROI measurements
  • Grading of performance; parameters (execution quality/branding/CI execution/creativity/brand tagging/interaction rates)
  • Tracking of “Talent soft skills”
06. License & Legal Management
Controlling

Long term compliance and license management

  • Contract archiving / usage rights overview and governance
  • Database setup for clients including rights alert mechanism (proactive indication of expiring rights etc.)
  • Prolongation of expiring rights through tool
  • Processing of ongoing inquiries on legal topics
  • Optional Talent Pool building

Working with us

We know how to take your events to the next level and create the unique and memorable experiences that will have you inspiring and connecting with your audience.

1. What are your objectives?
Initially, we seek to understand your specific needs to align our approach with your strategy. We can help you explore desired outcomes and establish key performance indicators that improve measurement and stakeholder reporting after the event.
2. What is the message you wish to convey?
Most events aim to communicate positive messages about an organization or initiative. We can transform your specific messages into an immersive experience, or help you identify and develop them during our planning process.
3. Who is your target audience?
We use your audience insights to create an event that surpasses expectations and achieves your goals. We also offer assistance in understanding your audience better through techniques like pre-event polls, questionnaires, and workshops.
4. What is your timescale and budget?
With a clear understanding of your goals and audience, we can efficiently utilize your budget and time to deliver optimal value. Our expertise lies in cost-effective solutions for both standard event requirements and unique challenges..
